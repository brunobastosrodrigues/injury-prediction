About the Project

Understanding and Preventing Injury Risks Through Artificial Intelligence

Project Overview

This project is part of my bachelor thesis (Bachelor in Computer Science, University of St. Gallen), focusing on using wearable health data to identify patterns associated with injury risk. By analyzing Garmin data, the goal is to train a machine learning model that can predict potential injury risks based on metrics such as heart rate, activity levels, sleep patterns, and more.

Why Your Participation Matters

Your contribution is invaluable in helping create a realistic, reliable and accurate model. The data you provide will be part of a collective dataset that enables the identification of meaningful patterns, which could eventually lead to better injury prevention strategies for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and coaches.
